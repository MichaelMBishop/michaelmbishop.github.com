Research Interests

My two active threads of research are:

  • numerical methods for uncertainty propagation in dynamical systems and
  • automated scheduling of high performance computations.

More broadly I am interested in dynamics, numerical linear algebra, symbolic representation of computation, and complex systems. I'm also curious but not knowledgeable about distributed computing, applied statistics, and automated scientific computing.

My background is in physics and engineering. Before academia I worked for a few years at an engineering firm developing structured light methods for 3d scanning. In physics I was interested in astronomy and gravitation.
